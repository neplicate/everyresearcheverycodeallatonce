About AlgoViz

AlgoViz is a personal project aimed at making data structures and algorithms accessible and engaging through interactive visualizations, challenges, and real-world applications.

My goal is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation, helping programmers at all levels deepen their understanding of fundamental computer science concepts.

Features

  • Interactive visualizations of key algorithms and data structures
  • Practice challenges with integrated code editor
  • Real-world projects demonstrating practical applications
  • Game-based learning through algorithm puzzles
  • Physical analogies to simplify complex concepts
